BD Land Acquisition Calculator — ARIPA 2017

About This Calculator

This app calculates land acquisition compensation under The Acquisition and Requisition of Immovable Property Act, 2017 (ARIPA 2017) of Bangladesh. All computations follow Section 9 of the Act.

Legal Basis — Section 9

Section 9(1)(a) — Market Value

Market value of property at date of Section 4 notice, based on 12-month average of similar properties in the vicinity.

Section 9(2) — Compensation Multiplier

Government acquisition: 200% of market value.
Non-Government: 300% of market value.

Section 9(1)(b)–(e) — Damages

  • (b) Standing crops & trees
  • (c) Severance damage
  • (d) Injurious affection to other property/earnings
  • (e) Relocation expenses

Section 9(3) — Additional 100%

An additional 100% compensation on all damages under (b), (c), (d), and (e). Effective damages = assessed amount × 2.

Compensation Formula
Step 1
Determine Market Value (MV) — avg. of similar properties over 12 months
Step 2
Land Compensation = MV × 200% (Govt) or MV × 300% (Non-Govt)
Step 3
Add damages: Crops/Trees + Severance + Injurious Affection + Relocation
Step 4
Enhanced Damages = Damages × 2 (100% original + 100% additional per Sec 9(3))
Step 5 — Total Award
Total = Land Compensation + Enhanced Damages
Section 10 — Excluded Matters
Degree of urgency leading to acquisition
Disinclination of owner to part with property
Damage that would not render a private person liable
Damage likely after Section 7 notice from future use
Increase in value from planned future use
Post-Section 4 alterations without DC sanction

Legal Disclaimers & Notes

1. Market value per Section 9(1)(a) based on 12-month average of similar properties preceding Section 4 notice date.

2. Government acquisition: 200% of MV [Section 9(2)]. Non-Government: 300% [Section 9(2) Proviso].

3. Additional 100% on all damage items per Section 9(3). Effective damages = 200% of assessed amounts.

4. Arbitrator's award shall not exceed DC's award by more than 10% [Section 33].

5. This calculator does NOT compute rehabilitation packages [Section 9(4)] or requisition compensation [Section 22].

6. Matters excluded per Section 10 are not part of this calculation.

This tool is for estimation purposes. The official award is determined by the Deputy Commissioner.
